Commands

-Fun commands-

>slap - Slaps the mention user. GIF command.

>kiss - Kisses the mentioned user. GIF command.

>hug - Hugs the mentioned user. GIF command.


-Moderation commands-

>kick - Kicks the mentioned user. Need permission: KICK_MEMBERS / needs channel: mod-logs.

>ban - Bans the mentioned user. Need permission: BAN_MEMBERS / needs channel: mod-logs.


-Other commands-

>ping - Shows the ping latency.

>botinfo - Shows the bot information.

>invite - Gives you the invite link to invite SensitiveBot.

>server - Gives you the support Discord server invite link of SensitiveBot.
